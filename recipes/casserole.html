Grandma's Ground Beef Casserole

Description

Nobody makes casserole like your Grandma. That's why this old-fashioned with egg noodles is always such a hit! It's made with ingredients you already have on hand (and, of course, a little bit of love).

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • White Sugar
  • Salt
  • Garlic Salt
  • Egg Noodles (or any pasta)
  • Sour Cream
  • Cream Cheese
  • Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese
