Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting. It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop, starting with a roux and adding milk, cheddar, and parmesan, resulting in a rich, decedant sauce that coats every nook and cranny of the noodles.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water until they are al dente. Transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce by melting butter, then whisking in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. Make the topping by melting two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese in a preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
  5. Enjoy your delicious homemade Mac and Cheese!
