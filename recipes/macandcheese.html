Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting. It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop, starting with a roux and adding milk, cheddar, and parmesan, resulting in a rich, decedant sauce that coats every nook and cranny of the noodles.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Bread Crumbs
